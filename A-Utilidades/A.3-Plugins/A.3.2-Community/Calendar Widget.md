# ⚙ Configuración

## General Settings

- Words per dot: *0*
- Start week on: `Monday`
- Confirm before creating new note: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Show week number: `TRUE`

## Advanced Settings

- Override locale: `Same as System` (Default)
