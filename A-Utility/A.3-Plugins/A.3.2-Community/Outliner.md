# ⚙️ Configuración

- Stick the cursor to the content: `Stick cursor out of Bullets` 
- Enhance Tab key: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Enhance the Enter key: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Vim-mode o/O insert bullets: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Enhance the <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>A</kbd> Behavior: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Improve the style of your lists: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Draw vertical indentation lines: `FALSE` *(Default)*
	- Vertical indentation click action: `Toggle Folding`
- Drag-and-Drop: `TRUE` *(Default)*
- Debug mode: `TRUE`
